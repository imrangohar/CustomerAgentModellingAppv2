AP SOP guidance:
- Intake must classify invoice, credit memo, debit memo, tax forms, statements, payment inquiries and remittance requests.
- Ambiguous documents go to Needs Review queue.
- For PO matching, hard stop mismatches beyond tolerance and route exceptions by discrepancy type.
- For non-PO coding, use category-to-account matrix with mandatory reason codes for overrides.
- For coding exceptions, route to controller queue with documented evidence.
